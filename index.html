Biography [CV]

I am currently a third year Ph.D. student in State Key Laboratory of Intelligent Technology and Systems, Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Jihong Zhu. Previously, I received the M. S and B. S degree from School of Astronautics in Beihang University (former Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics) in 2015 and 2012, under the supervision of Prof. Yunfeng Dong.

My research interests are in the area of guidance and control of vehicles, in particular missiles and unmanned aircraft. Specific interests are: cooperative guidance, aircraft control, intelligent control, and Sensor fusion.

Recently, I am working on the research of active aircraft protection problem using computational methods and machine learning.
